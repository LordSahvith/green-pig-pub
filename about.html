About

What The 4th Pig Built

The Green Pig Pub strives to be an eco-friendly pub that is truly green and has more to offer than any other. We've got a delicious food menu, which accommodates all, including vegan and gluten free diets. But that's not all we have to offer, we have live music 2 nights a week, karaoke and group trivia events that get everyone using their noggins. You can't find any better pub, especially with the amazing staff that really makes you feel like a V.I.P. every night you come to hang out. It's a great place to meet up with friends or make new ones. Centrally located downtown within walking distance of the Salt Palace, Grand America & all other downtown destinations.
