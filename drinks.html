Here at The Green Pig Pub we always supply you with the best of the best, which means we've got everything from beer to cocktails and everything in between. We support our local breweries like Uinta, Epic & Wasatch. However, we do carry domestic and import beers as well as the new Rotator draft, which changes with every keg so to keep things exciting. If beers aren't your thing, fear not because we've got all the spirits you want. Tequilla, whiskey, rum, gin...you name it! Not a fan of shots? Mix it up and have yourself a wonderful cocktail instead or try one of our amazing house recipes. Last but not least we have a great assortment of wines for those wine connoisseurs out there. We've got both reds and whites to fill your tastes. Come down and enjoy your evening with a great drink!
